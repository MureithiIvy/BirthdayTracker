Birthday-Calculator

This website gives you the exact day of the week you were born and your AKAN name when you enter your date,month and the year that you were born.
AKAN is a Ghanian name which is derived from Ghanian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds
to the day in the week they were born,below are steps to follow when entering your data;

  1. Enter the exact date of your birthday e.g 28
  2. Key in your birthday month e.g 08
  3. Enter the exact year of your birthday e.g 1998
