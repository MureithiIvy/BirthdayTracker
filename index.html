Birthday Tracker

Hello there? Would you want to know the exact day of the week you were born? Well, then this site will help you achieve that. Most importantly it also outputs your AKAN name. You are probably wondering what AKAN names are.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds
to the day in the week they were born,and that is there AKAN name. All you need to do here is to enter the date,month and year that you were born.

    Please follow this simple instructions.

  1. Enter the exact date of your birthday
  2. Key in your birthday month e.g 08
  3. Enter the exact year of your birthday e.g 1998
